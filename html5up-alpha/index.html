Taipei and I

Born and raised in Taipei, I'm profoundly influenced by its fast-pace. Though many people consider Taipei as an indifferent city, I still love Taipei so much since it is my hometown. I currently live in Da-An District with my parents, however, my favorite place in Taipei is Xin-Yi District, which is where Taipei 101 locates. Whenever I see Taipei 101 and the tourists amazed by its magnificence, I feel so proud to be a Taiwanese.

My Hobbies

I love watching TV series and YouTube videos. My favorite TV Series of all time is definetely Game of Thrones. It is an adaption of A Song of Ice and Fire, George R. R. Martin's fantasy novel. However, my current favorite is a Spanish TV series, La Casa de Papel, also known as Money Heist. Stranger Things, Revenge are also on my recommended list.
And my favorite YouTuber is a Polish American, Claudia Sulewski. I have been following her for over three years. Her positive and hardworking attitude toward all things have influenced me a lot, makes me want to become a better person just like her.
